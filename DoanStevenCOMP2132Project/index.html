How to Play

Players roll a pair of dice 3 times. After the third roll, the player with the highest score wins! After a game has ended with the pop-up message showing, you can click 'Reset Game' to play again!

Scoring Rules

  • If any of the player's or computer's two dice comes up as a 1, the score for that round is 0.
  • If the player rolls a pair of the same numbers, the score is the total of the two dice times 2. (EX: a player rolls 2 and 2, their score is (2 + 2) * 2 = 8)
  • If the player rolls any other combination, the score is the total value of the two dice. (EX: a player rolls 3 and 4, their score is 7.
